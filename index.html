They use Jeyser CRM

By Juniors, for Juniors

Jeyser is the result of 5 years of work between M-Gate & N7 Consulting, both belonging to the France Junior-Entreprise top 30. Jeyser is rated with a platinum medal by SensioLabs, which certifies its quality (from a developer point of view).

Here are a few points which seems important to us:

Not commercial. We are not looking for your money. Book a server, pay for it monthly and that's all. Use the full potential of your tools, don't be limited by prices

Updated and maintained. Jeyser is used on a daily basis by several junior-entreprise. Bugs are quickly fixed once you signaled them.

Relies on professionnal technologies. Such as Docker and Symfony for a better security.

Keep the control of your data. Everything is hosted on your server, your control over your data is complete. Use Jeyser as a data source for all your creative projects

Open to contributions. Something is missing on Jeyser or you have a tremendeous idea ? Share it with us on Github and we'll make Jeyser a better product for everyone

Free forever. Our licence ensures you that Jeyser will remain open-source forever.
